Hotel Rules

Family-Oriented Policy

Since our hotel is a family-oriented hotel, you cannot enter here without a marriage certificate. This rule only applies to citizens of Uzbekistan. One more thing we should mention: if one partner is an Uzbek citizen and the other is a foreign citizen, we also cannot allow them to stay.

Quiet Hours

After 11:00 PM, please avoid making noise. Remember that other guests are also resting.

No Smoking Policy

Smoking tobacco products in the room is prohibited. Out of respect for the next guest, we kindly ask you not to smoke in the room. We have fines for this!

Room Key Policy

Please keep the room key card safe and do not lose it. Otherwise, you will be charged a $10 penalty.

Room Items Policy

Nothing in the room is for sale. Please do not remove items you like from the room and put yourself and us in an awkward situation!

Following these rules ensures a pleasant stay for everyone. We appreciate your cooperation.

Respectfully, the Old Tashkent Hotel & SPA Team
